About

Bio and approach

I am a sociologist trained in qualitative inquiry and the analysis of contemporary imaginaries. After completing doctoral research on transhumanism and a postdoctoral fellowship, I now work across research, teaching, public speaking, and consulting.

My work helps organizations think clearly about AI and emerging technologies, not only as tools, but as social environments that reshape attention, professional identity, governance, and meaning.
